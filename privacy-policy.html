Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026

We believe in full transparency. This page explains exactly what data Shalaye accesses, why, and what we never do with it.

✅ We do not sell or share your data

📖 Overview

Shalaye is a Chrome browser extension that uses artificial intelligence to help users summarize, simplify, and understand web content. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information when you use the extension.

The short version: Shalaye reads the text on the page you're viewing (only when you click "Summarize") and sends it to an AI API to generate a summary. Nothing is stored on our servers. Nothing is sold. Ever.

🔍 What Data We Access

Shalaye only accesses the following, and only when you explicitly trigger an action:

  • Website content (page text): When you click "Summarize This Page" or use the Quick Explain feature, the visible text content of the current webpage is extracted and sent to the AI API to generate a summary.
  • Page title and URL: Used to label your saved summaries so you can identify them later. Stored locally in your browser only.
  • Theme preference (light/dark): Stored locally in your browser using Chrome Storage so your preference is remembered.
  • Saved summaries: If you choose to save a summary, it is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage. We never upload saved summaries to any server.

🚫 What We Do NOT Collect

  • ❌ We do not collect your name, email, or any personal identity information
  • ❌ We do not track your browsing history or which sites you visit
  • ❌ We do not use cookies or any cross-site tracking
  • ❌ We do not collect health, financial, or authentication data
  • ❌ We do not monitor your keystrokes, mouse activity, or screen
  • ❌ We do not collect location or IP address information
  • ❌ We do not have a backend server that stores any of your data

🤖 AI API Usage

To generate summaries and explanations, Shalaye sends the text content of the page you are currently viewing to third-party AI services:

  • Google Gemini API (Primary): Page text is sent to Google's Gemini API for processing. Google's data handling is governed by their Privacy Policy and Generative AI Terms.
  • Groq API (Fallback): If the primary API is unavailable, page text may be sent to Groq's API. Groq's data handling is governed by their Privacy Policy.
⚠️ Important: Do not use the Summarize feature on pages containing sensitive personal information (passwords, medical records, financial data, etc.), as that text would be sent to a third-party AI service for processing.

💾 Local Storage Only

Any data that Shalaye stores (saved summaries, your theme preference) is kept exclusively on your own device using Chrome's built-in chrome.storage.local API. This data:

  • Never leaves your device (except as noted in AI API usage above)
  • Is not accessible to us or any third party
  • Can be deleted at any time by removing the extension

🔐 Chrome Permissions Explained

  • activeTab: Allows Shalaye to read the content of the tab you are currently viewing — only when you click the extension.
  • storage: Allows saving your summaries and preferences locally on your device.
  • scripting: Allows Shalaye to inject the Quick Explain tooltip into the page when you select text.
  • contextMenus: Adds the "Shalaye: Explain this" option when you right-click selected text.

👶 Children's Privacy

Shalaye is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has used this extension in a way that raises privacy concerns, please contact us.

📝 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
